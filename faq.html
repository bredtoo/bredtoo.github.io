Frequently Asked Questions!

For if you have any questions!

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First make your way over to mynetpay.nl and log in using your school name and password. Make sure you have money on your account! Paper doesn't grow on trees! (wait. Does it?) If not, you can go to a pinpoint at the ground floor of HZ Middelburg, or just press the "Top Up Now" button. Now that you have money in your account, press the "Mobility Print" option. Click your desired OS and run the downloaded file. The papercut printer client should automatically run and install the printer drivers. Now go to your designated file that you, oh so want to print, and select HZ_printer. Now you can go to a printer located at the HZ building, tap your card and select the files you sent! Happy printing!

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Go to one of the printers and put your student card on the card reader. Lift up the hatch and place your desired document on the glass or in the document feeder. On the touchscreen choose the scan option and then select which option suits best for you! The options vary from USB but if you don't have a thumb drive on you, e-mail is always an option!

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Log in to your portal! Then under your personal menu you should see the webshop tab. If you don't see this, don't stress! Click "Change personal menu" and press the green plus button on webshop. Now you can access the webshop page and spend to your heart's desire! To go directly to the webshop page, please click me!

How can you book a project space?

Go to the Self Service Portal and click the reservations tab. Then click on "Reserve a meeting or project room". Select an available timeslot and add the required information. Confirm your reservation and you're done!

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

When you reach the barriers, use your student card to open them, as the card also functions as your parking pass. In Vlissingen you can park directly under or next to the HZ Toren, or in the general HZ parking lot beside the building, following the on-site signs. In Middelburg, parking at the building is limited to visitor and disabled spaces, so for longer stays you can request an in/out card for the nearby garage at the service desk. If the barrier does not open or you need special access, contact the HZ reception or facilities team for assistance!
