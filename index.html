Why I chose the HBO-ICT programme.

After graduating from highschool, I originally thought I'd go in the film industry or be a biologist or something like that. When I took my gap year I dabbled in content creation and I realised it just didn't really interest me anymore. So when it came to the end of my gap year I looked back at my strengths, especially in computer science, and was encouraged by a friend studying at HZ University, I discovered ICT as a path that aligned with my interests. The school had a nice atmosphere, strong first impressions, and the chance to explore multiple IT fields. It convinced me to apply, and since starting, I've found a real passion for front-end development and coding in general. If you wish to read more about why I chose this, please read the blog I wrote where i go more in depth about it!
